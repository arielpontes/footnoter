Footnoter

Write your text with inline references like this:

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet[ref]my first reference[/ref], consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua[ref]my second reference[/ref]. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat.

[reflist]

* Bold used for visual effect only, your text doesn't actually have to be bold.

The output should look like this:

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet[1], consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua[2]. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat.

  2. my second reference
